# Expanded Access (Compassionate Use) Compliance Kit: Ectoparasitic Pathology

## Overview
This repository contains standardized templates for clinicians and hospitals seeking **Expanded Access (Compassionate Use)** authorization for investigational ectoparasite treatments. These documents are designed to align with **FDA 21 CFR 312.310** and **Form FDA 3926** requirements.

## Workflow for Hospital Administrators & Clinicians
1.  **Download & Customize:** Clone this repo and fill in the bracketed sections of the Protocol and Consent Form.
2.  **Submit to IRB:** Submit `01_PROTOCOL.md` and `02_INFORMED_CONSENT.md` to your local Institutional Review Board.
3.  **Request Authorization (The Supply Step):** To submit to the FDA, you require a **Letter of Authorization (LOA)** and Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) data.
    *   **[Click here to request your LOA, Supply, and Clinical Training Kit]**
4.  **Submit to FDA:** Once you receive the LOA from us, attach it to **Form FDA 3926** and submit via the CDER NextGen Portal.
